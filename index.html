FAQs

Find answers to common loan-related questions and get clarity on eligibility, interest rates, repayment, and more.


1. What types of loans can I apply for?

You can apply for personal loans, home loans, car loans, and education loans based on your needs.

2. What are the eligibility criteria for a loan?

Eligibility depends on your credit score, income, employment status, and other financial factors.

3. How can I check my loan eligibility?

You can use our loan eligibility calculator or contact our support team for assistance.

4. What is the interest rate on loans?

Interest rates vary depending on the type of loan, your credit profile, and market conditions.

5. How do I repay my loan?

Loans can be repaid through EMIs via bank transfers, auto-debit, or online payment portals.

6. Can I prepay my loan?

Yes, you can prepay your loan, but some loans may have prepayment penalties.
